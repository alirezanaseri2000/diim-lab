# Design Innovation and Intelligent Manufacturing (DIIM) Lab

Official website for the **Design Innovation and Intelligent Manufacturing (DIIM) Lab** at the University of Guelph.

Design Innovation and Intelligent Manufacturing (DIIM) Lab focuses on synergistic research on AI, digital twins, and human-centered design. We are part of the [School of Engineering](https://www.uoguelph.ca/engineering/) at the [University of Guelph](https://www.uoguelph.ca/), Guelph, Ontario, Canada.
