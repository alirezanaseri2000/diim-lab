We are excited to announce that our project on **AI for soilless growing media** has been funded by the **NSERC Alliance Grant**. This project will leverage artificial intelligence and data-driven approaches to optimize growing conditions in soilless agricultural systems, contributing to more sustainable and efficient food production. Congratulations to the team!
